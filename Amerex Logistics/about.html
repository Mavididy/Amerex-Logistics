About Amerex

Delivering Beyond Borders Since 2010

We are a global logistics company committed to connecting businesses worldwide with reliable, efficient, and innovative shipping solutions.

OUR STORY

How We Started

Founded in 2010, Amerex began with a simple mission: to make global logistics accessible, transparent, and reliable for businesses of all sizes. What started as a small freight forwarding company in Lagos has grown into a trusted international logistics partner serving clients across 25+ countries.

Over the years, we've invested heavily in technology, infrastructure, and partnerships to ensure that every shipment—whether by sea, land, rail, or air—reaches its destination safely and on time. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of over 5,000 businesses worldwide.

Today, Amerex stands as a symbol of reliability in the logistics industry, combining traditional values of customer service with cutting-edge tracking technology and sustainable shipping practices.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional logistics services that empower businesses to grow globally, while maintaining the highest standards of safety, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted logistics partner, recognized for innovation, sustainability, and our unwavering commitment to connecting businesses across borders.
