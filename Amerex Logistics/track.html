‚ùì Tracking FAQs

Where can I find my tracking number?

Your tracking number is in the confirmation email we sent when your shipment was created. You can also find it on your receipt or shipping label.

How often is tracking information updated?

Tracking information is updated in real-time as your package moves through our network. Expect updates at major checkpoints throughout the delivery journey.

What if my tracking number isn't working?

If your tracking number isn't found, please wait 24 hours after shipping for it to appear in our system. If issues persist, contact our support team at +234 800 263 7391.

Can I track multiple packages at once?

Yes! Simply enter each tracking number one at a time. You can also create a free account to track all your shipments in one dashboard.
