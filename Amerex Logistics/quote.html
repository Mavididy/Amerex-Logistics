Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the online quotes?

Online quotes are close estimates and include standard fees. Final pricing is confirmed at pickup and can change slightly based on actual weight/dimensions and surcharges.

Can I modify my shipment after getting a quote?

Yes – you can modify details before confirming booking. Changes after confirmation may incur additional fees.

How long is my quote valid?

Quotes are valid for 30 days from generation. Fuel surcharges and other fees may change after this period.

Are there any hidden fees?

No – we display standard fees. Additional optional services (signature, insurance, Saturday delivery) are shown clearly in the form.

Do you offer packaging services?

Yes, we offer professional packaging services for an additional fee. Our experts will ensure your items are properly secured using industry-standard materials to prevent damage during transit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For business accounts, we also offer invoicing options with approved credit terms.
