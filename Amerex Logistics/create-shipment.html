Shipping Tips

Packaging Guidelines

  • Use sturdy, double-walled boxes for heavy items
  • Leave 2 inches of cushioning around items
  • Secure internal items to prevent shifting
  • Use H-tape method on all seams for added strength

Cost-Saving Tips

  • Consolidate multiple items into one package
  • Consider economy service for non-urgent shipments
  • Schedule regular pickups for volume discounts
  • Use our packaging materials for optimal rates

Customs Information

  • Declare accurate value to avoid penalties
  • Include detailed descriptions of all items
  • Research restricted items for destination country
  • Include all required documentation to avoid delays
